Numbo-Maze Instructions:

  1. Select Difficulty:

    2. Choose your preferred difficulty level: Easy or Hard.

  2. Choose Your Character:

    3. Pick your favorite character for a personalized gaming experience.

  3. Start the Game:

    4. Use your keyboard's arrow keys to guide your character to the pixelated Math Brains on the screen.

  4. Meet Math Brains:

    5. Throughout the maze, you'll encounter Math Brains. These are tiny pixelated brains with math questions. Each Math Brain presents a math question. Answer correctly to progress and enhance your math skills.

  5. Mistakes Happen:

    6. If you get a question wrong, don't worry. The correct answer and a solution example will be shown. You have three lives for each maze.

  6. Timer

    7. Keep an eye on the timer in the top right corner. Finish your questions in the allotted time to pass the level.

  7. Options After Winning / Losing:

    8. Reach the allowed wrong attempts or run out of time? Choose to restart the maze or return to the home screen. Win and face the same options. If you win in the Easy Level, you may choose to progress to the Hard Level.

  8. Have Fun and Learn:

    9. Enjoy the maze, tackle math challenges, and improve your skills along the way!
